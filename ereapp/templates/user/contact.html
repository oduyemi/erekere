Contact Us

We organise group tours, school excursions, corporate retreats, holiday packages and also offer tour guide services within Africa, especially in Nigeria to educate people about the beauty within.

We stand behind your promise to provide reliable tourism services that offers luxury experience at pocket friendly prices. Thereby, curating experiences that linger.

Our Customers

We provide educational, cultural, adventure and recreational tourism services for individuals, organizations, government parastatals and businesses. In our hospitality division, we currently manage a 91-room resort in Southwest Nigeria. Erékéré was birthed from pure passion and an opportunity to provide a service that many desired but couldn't access. Nigeria and the rest of Africa has a massive latent tourism potential that needs to be exploited and we are putting ourselves in a position to drive this in the future.
