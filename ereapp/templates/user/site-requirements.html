Site Requirements

Local tourism is very important for any country to grow, it should be taken seriously.

Supported Browsers

This website is optimized to run on the following minimum browser requirements.

  • Edge 12+
  • Internet Explorer 10+
  • Safari 9+
  • Firefox 40+
  • Chrome 50+

If you are running an older version of one of these browsers, you may download the latest version by visiting their website or updating the browser.

Screen Resolutions

For ease of viewing and a better overall browsing experience, we recommend that your screen resolution be set to one of the following:

  • 1024x768 (recommended)
  • 1920x1200
  • 800x600

JavaScript and Cookies

We utilize JavaScript and first-party cookies throughout our site to provide you an enhanced user experience and to measure anonymous data on how users interact with our websites. Our gift catalog, order checkout, honor card process, and more use JavaScript and cookies. Our website may not function properly, or at all, if JavaScript and cookies are disabled. Allowing these features is a setting on your browser. Please refer to your browser's help section for instructions to change these settings, as each browser is different.

We utilize third-party cookies in order to provide you a more personalized experience on our website and to enable us to provide personalized advertisements on other websites. This allows us to let you know about urgent fundraising needs and special match opportunities.
